VOTING QUEST

This game is all about teaching young kids about the process of voting!

Now more than ever, it's important for everyone to vote. Young people, who are a historically under-represented group when it comes to voting, should be informed at a young age about the voting process. That's exactly what this game is for!

This game was made in 24 hours by Tyler Wong, Caroline Johnson, Jonathan Beaubien, and Nora Morsi for Dubhacks 2020, made with emotional support from Nick Roberts and Zahra McKee.
