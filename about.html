VOTING QUEST

Learn how to fulfill your civic duty!

This game is all about teaching young kids about the process of voting!

We believe in the importance of having a voice in your government, and since young people are a repeatedly hard group to get out to vote, our team thought creating a game targetting young children that would teach them the importance of the voting process.

This game was made in 24 hours by Tyler Wong, Caroline Johnson, Jonathan Beaubien, and Nora Morsi for Dubhacks 2020, made with emotional support from Nick Roberts and Zahra McKee.
